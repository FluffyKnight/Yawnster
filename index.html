About

YAWNster is an innovative company where its core vision is that people need better naps throughout the day to stay productive. We provide that need to help people recharge so that they can feel better and be better. We want you to live the life that you dream of an imagine with our affordable and accommodating services.

The Co-founders, Kenny Leung & Rubin Richardson, whom were friends in college realized that transition from the college to the working world that its challenging and they felt tired, sleepy, loss of focus, and more importantly, lost in productivity. This faze not only affected their professional lives, it carried over to their own personal lives too. They wondered, if it affects them then it must affect others as well. That is when they realized that there was need and their decision to fill that need came about.

Let us help you by booking your reservation for one of our state of the art resting facilities within New York or contact us for more details via our online form or via email:support@yawnster.com or via phone: (212) 427- 1184.
